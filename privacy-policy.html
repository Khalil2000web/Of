PRIVACY POLICY
THIS PRIVACY POLICY WAS LAST REVISED AND IS EFFECTIVE AS OF JUNE 19, 2024.
1. INTRODUCTION - THIS PRIVACY POLICY EXPLAINS HOW KHALIIIL.COM COLLECTS, USES, AND PROTECTS PERSONAL INFORMATION WHEN YOU VISIT OUR WEBSITE.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
2.A INFORMATION YOU GIVE US - IN ADDITION TO THE NAME PROVIDED WHEN ACCESSING THE PRIVATE PAGE, USERS MAY HAVE THE OPTION TO VOLUNTARILY PROVIDE THEIR GMAIL ADDRESS FOR EMAIL SUBSCRIPTIONS. THIS INFORMATION IS COLLECTED SOLELY FOR THE PURPOSE OF SENDING UPDATES AND COMMUNICATIONS FROM KHALIIIL.COM AND WILL NOT BE SHARED WITH ANY THIRD PARTIES.

2.B COMMENTS AND INTERACTIONS - WHEN USERS LEAVE COMMENTS ON BLOG POSTS OR INTERACT WITH THE WEBSITE, WE COLLECT THE DATA SHOWN IN THE COMMENTS FORM, AND ALSO THE USER’S IP ADDRESS AND BROWSER USER AGENT STRING TO HELP SPAM DETECTION.

3. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?
3.A PERSONAL INFORMATION - THE NAME PROVIDED IS SOLELY USED FOR IDENTIFICATION PURPOSES TO GRANT ACCESS TO THE PRIVATE PAGE. WE DO NOT SHARE THIS INFORMATION WITH ANY THIRD PARTIES.

3.B EMAIL ADDRESSES - IF YOU PROVIDE YOUR GMAIL ADDRESS FOR EMAIL SUBSCRIPTIONS, WE WILL USE IT TO SEND YOU UPDATES AND COMMUNICATIONS FROM KHALIIIL.COM.

3.C COMMENTS AND INTERACTIONS - COMMENTS AND OTHER INTERACTIONS MAY BE USED TO ENGAGE WITH USERS, RESPOND TO FEEDBACK, AND IMPROVE THE CONTENT ON THE WEBSITE.

4. SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION - WE DO NOT SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES EXCEPT AS NECESSARY TO COMPLY WITH APPLICABLE LAWS AND REGULATIONS, TO RESPOND TO A SUBPOENA, SEARCH WARRANT OR OTHER LAWFUL REQUEST FOR INFORMATION WE RECEIVE, OR TO OTHERWISE PROTECT OUR RIGHTS.

5. CONTENT AND CREDITS
5.A PHOTOS ANY PHOTOS SHARED ON KHALIIIL.COM ARE OWNED BY THE SITE OWNER UNLESS OTHERWISE CREDITED. IF YOU WISH TO USE ANY OF THESE PHOTOS, PLEASE CONTACT US FOR PERMISSION.

5.B MUSIC - MUSIC SHARED ON THE SITE INCLUDES PROPER CREDITS AND LINKS TO THE ORIGINAL ARTISTS IN ACCORDANCE WITH COPYRIGHT LAWS. IF YOU NOTICE ANY ERRORS IN CREDITS OR LINKS, PLEASE CONTACT US TO RECTIFY THE ISSUE.

5.C TRAVEL AND BLOG CONTENT - TRAVEL EXPERIENCES AND BLOG POSTS FEATURED ON KHALIIIL.COM ARE PERSONAL REFLECTIONS OF THE SITE OWNER. THESE STORIES TAKE READERS ON A JOURNEY THROUGH THE EYES OF THE AUTHOR, SHARING INTIMATE DETAILS, INSIGHTS, AND EMOTIONS ENCOUNTERED DURING THEIR TRAVELS. FROM BREATHTAKING LANDSCAPES TO CULTURAL IMMERSIONS, EACH POST OFFERS A UNIQUE PERSPECTIVE AND INVITES READERS TO EXPLORE THE WORLD THROUGH PERSONAL NARRATIVES.

6. SECURITY - WE TAKE APPROPRIATE MEASURES TO ENSURE THE SECURITY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION. ACCESS TO THE PRIVATE PAGE IS PROTECTED BY A PASSWORD, AND WE REGULARLY REVIEW OUR SECURITY PROTOCOLS TO SAFEGUARD YOUR DATA. COMMENTS AND OTHER INTERACTIONS ARE MONITORED FOR SPAM AND MALICIOUS ACTIVITY.

7. YOUR RIGHTS - YOU HAVE THE RIGHT TO ACCESS PERSONAL INFORMATION WE HOLD ABOUT YOU AND TO ASK THAT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION BE CORRECTED, UPDATED, OR DELETED. IF YOU WOULD LIKE TO EXERCISE THIS RIGHT, PLEASE CONTACT US THROUGH THE CONTACT INFORMATION BELOW.

8. DATA RETENTION - WHEN YOU PROVIDE US WITH PERSONAL INFORMATION, WE WILL MAINTAIN THIS INFORMATION FOR OUR RECORDS UNLESS AND UNTIL YOU ASK US TO DELETE THIS INFORMATION.

9. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY - THE SITE IS INTENDED FOR USERS UNDER THE AGE OF 10. WE TAKE SPECIAL CARE TO PROTECT THE PRIVACY OF YOUNG USERS. WE DO NOT KNOWINGLY COLLECT PERSONAL INFORMATION FROM CHILDREN UNDER 10 WITHOUT PARENTAL CONSENT. IF WE BECOME AWARE THAT WE HAVE INADVERTENTLY RECEIVED PERSONAL INFORMATION FROM A CHILD UNDER THE AGE OF 10 WITHOUT VERIFICATION OF PARENTAL CONSENT, WE WILL DELETE THE INFORMATION FROM OUR RECORDS.

NONETHELESS, WE SEEK TO PROTECT THE INTEGRITY OF OUR SITE AND WELCOME ANY FEEDBACK ABOUT THESE SITES.
