1. INTRODUCTION
Welcome to Khaliiil.com. At Khaliiil.com, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring a secure online experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website. By using Khaliiil.com, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us using the details provided at the end of this policy.
2. Information We Collect
2.1. Information Provided by Users

  • Join Page Form: When you voluntarily provide your name and email address through our join page form, this data is collected to facilitate the sending of updates, newsletters, and notifications about new features, improvements, and significant changes related to Khaliiil.com. This form is managed by Formspree.io, a third-party service provider specializing in form management. Formspree.io’s practices regarding the handling and storage of personal data are detailed in their Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review their policy for comprehensive information on how your data is managed.
  • Email Communications: If you choose to subscribe to our mailing list, your email address will be used to send you newsletters, promotional materials, and other updates relevant to Khaliiil.com. You may opt out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link included in each email. We do not use your email address for purposes other than those described in this policy.

2.2. Information Not Collected

  • User Comments and Interactions: Currently, Khaliiil.com does not feature a comment section, forum, or any interactive elements that allow users to post content or provide feedback beyond the join page form. Therefore, we do not collect or process data related to user comments, posts, or any other interactive contributions.

3. Use of Your Information
3.1. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Personal Data Utilization: The information collected through the join page form, including your name and email address, is used solely for the purpose of sending you updates, newsletters, and information related to Khaliiil.com.
